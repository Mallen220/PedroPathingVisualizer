# Getting Started

Welcome to the Pedro Pathing Visualizer! This tool helps you create and visualize paths for your robot.

## Basics

1. **Create a Path**: Use the tools on the left to add points and create a path.
2. **Edit Points**: Drag points to adjust their position.
3. **Visualize**: Press play to see your robot follow the path.

## Tips

- Use the shortcuts to speed up your workflow.
- Check the settings to customize the visualization.
